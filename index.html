Who We Are

Built for the Northeast,
Built for Everyone

NEST Cluster is a government-backed, multi-institutional science and technology platform bridging the gap between research, industry and community across all eight states of Northeast India.

  • ✓ Accessible programming in regional languages and in-person formats for rural communities
  • ✓ Partnerships with IITs, NITs, state universities, and private research institutes
  • ✓ Transparent, merit-based grant disbursement with community representation
  • ✓ Indigenous knowledge integration with modern S&T methodologies

What We Do

Core Focus Areas

Six pillars driving sustainable S&T development across the Northeast region.

01
🌾

Agri-Tech & Food Security

Precision agriculture, crop genomics, cold-chain innovation, and indigenous crop revival for sustainable farming across NE India's diverse geographies.

02
🌿

Biodiversity & Environment

Conservation science, climate resilience research, traditional ecological knowledge documentation, and ecological monitoring systems.

03
💻

Digital & Deep Tech

AI/ML applications, rural connectivity infrastructure, digital health platforms, and language technology for NE Indian languages.

04
⚕️

Health & Biomedicine

Endemic disease research, telemedicine platforms, traditional medicine studies, and community health innovation for remote populations.

05
⚡

Clean Energy

Micro-hydro power systems, solar micro-grids, biogas from agricultural waste, and energy access solutions for off-grid communities.

06
🏭

Industry & Enterprise

S&T-led entrepreneurship, startup incubation, MSME technology upgradation, and supply chain innovation for NE's unique industry base.

What We Offer

Our Programmes

From foundational research grants to intensive startup acceleration, NEST offers pathways for every stage of your innovation journey.

Flagship • Research

NEST Core Research Fund

Competitive grants of ₹15–75 lakhs for fundamental and applied research in NEST's six focus areas. Open to researchers at accredited institutions.

Applications Open →
Collaborative • Research

Cross-State Research Consortium

Multi-institution collaborative research up to ₹2 Cr, requiring at least 3 institutions across 2 different NE states. Priority for climate and health themes.

Round 3 Open →
Early Career • Research

Young Scientist Fellowship

Two-year fellowships for PhD holders under 35 years with a monthly stipend, lab access, and mentorship from senior NEST network researchers.

Closing Soon →
Flagship • Startups

NEST Accelerator Programme

Intensive 20-week accelerator with seed funding up to ₹25 lakhs, mentorship, investor access, and co-working space for science-based startups.

Cohort 4 Open →
Rural Tech • Startups

Grassroots Innovation Challenge

Support for grassroots innovators and rural entrepreneurs with technology at core. No formal qualification required. Problem-first, innovation-led.

Always Open →
Deep Tech • Startups

DeepTech NE Fund

Equity-free grants and co-investment for deep technology startups in AI, biotech, advanced materials and space tech from the Northeast.

Next Round: Q2 2025 →
Skilling • Community

Rural Technology Literacy

Free digital and science literacy training for rural communities, delivered in local languages through a network of 60+ community resource centres.

Ongoing →
Academic • Training

Faculty Research Development

Short-term research training, international exposure programmes, and equipment sharing network for faculty at NE's state universities and colleges.

Leadership • Executive

Science Leadership Summit

Annual 3-day residential summit for science and technology leaders from the Northeast — policymakers, researchers, entrepreneurs and community leaders.
