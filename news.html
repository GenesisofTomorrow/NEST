NEST-Funded Team Develops Low-Cost Water Purification Technology for Flood-Prone Districts

A research team from Tezpur University, supported by NEST's Core Research Fund, has successfully trialled a ceramic-based water filter deployable within 24 hours in disaster-affected zones. The technology, developed over 18 months, can purify 10,000 litres daily at a cost of ₹400 per unit.
