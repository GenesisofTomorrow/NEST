Assam led all states with 38% of total traffic (9,436 visitors), followed by Manipur (11%, 2,731), Arunachal Pradesh (12%, 2,980), Meghalaya (10%, 2,483), Nagaland (9%), Tripura (8%), Mizoram (7%), and Sikkim (5%).

Rural access grew to 34% of total sessions (+12% MoM), with 78% of rural visitors using mobile devices — reaffirming the importance of mobile-first design.

Top Pages & Content

Programmes page received highest traffic (42% click-through from home), followed by News & Events (28%), About (18%), and Contact (12%). The February Grant announcement drove a 340% spike in traffic on 8 Feb.

Traffic Sources

Organic search drove 44% of visitors, direct access 28%, social media 14%, referrals from partner institutions 9%, and email campaigns 5%.
